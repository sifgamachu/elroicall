El Roi Call

El Roi — "the God who sees me" — is the name Hagar gave God in the desert, in Genesis sixteen, at the moment she felt most invisible. El Roi Call was built on that verse: a line you can call when you're carrying something heavy, and be met by a voice who has lived what you're living.

El Roi Call pairs timeless biblical witness with careful modern technology — AI companion voices inspired by figures like Job, Esther, Ruth, and Peter — to offer spiritual encouragement, prayer, and reflection. It is a guided spiritual experience, not therapy or counseling, and every design decision is made with the care of a caller's trust in mind.
